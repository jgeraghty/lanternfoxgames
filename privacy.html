1. Information We Collect

We may collect or receive the following categories of information:

  • Device and usage data: Device identifiers, device model, OS version, language settings, gameplay events, and performance diagnostics.
  • Advertising data: Information used by ad providers to deliver ads and measure ad performance (including ad identifiers, where permitted).
  • Purchase data: Purchase transaction details for in-app purchases processed by your app store platform.

We do not currently provide account registration in Double Up, and we do not intentionally collect direct identifiers such as your full name unless you contact us.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain the Game and core gameplay features
  • Serve, measure, and improve advertising experiences
  • Process and verify in-app purchases
  • Analyze technical performance and troubleshoot errors
  • Protect against abuse, fraud, and violations of our Terms

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us run the Game (for example, ad and purchase infrastructure partners)
  • Platform providers such as Apple for payment and app distribution operations
  • Legal or safety parties when required by law or to protect users, rights, and property

We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense of exchanging data for money. Certain ad-related data sharing may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" under California law depending on implementation.
