The game

Double Up

A level-based number merge puzzle where you tap or drag adjacent matching tiles to combine them. Reach target values within a move budget, earn up to three stars per level, and unlock new arcs as you progress. Easy to learn — deeply satisfying to master.

  • ◆
    Three game modes. Standard puzzles, Frozen boards with spreading ice to thaw, and Delivery challenges where you guide a tile to a goal.
  • ◆
    Daily challenges. A fresh 6×6 puzzle every day with leaderboards and streaks.
  • ◆
    Powerups & progression. Earn tools like Undo, Swap, and Shatter as you clear levels and collect stars.

The studio

Built on arcade instincts

LanternFox Games is a small indie studio with a simple belief: the best games respect your time and reward your attention.

We grew up on the kind of games that pulled you in with a simple idea and kept you there with invisible depth. Double Up is our first release — a number merge puzzle campaign built to be picked up in thirty seconds and played for an hour.

We prototype quickly, tune relentlessly, and ship only when it feels right. No filler. No roadmap theater.
